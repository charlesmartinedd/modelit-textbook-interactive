Unit 2: Living Systems

The Human Body as a System

Every 7 years, you have an almost completely new body. How does 'you' stay you?

You are a walking ecosystem. Your body is made of 37 trillion cells, all working together to keep you alive. But they don't just work randomly. They work to maintain Balance.

Homeostasis: The Balancing Act

The fancy word for this balance is Homeostasis. It's like a thermostat in your house, but for everything: temperature, salt, sugar, water, oxygen.

Negative Feedback Loops

Notice that when you get too hot, you sweat to cool down. When you get too cold, you shiver to warm up. This is a Negative Feedback Loop. "Negative" doesn't mean bad‚Äîit means it reverses the change to bring you back to normal.

What Happens When It Breaks?

If your body can't produce Insulin (the signal to lower sugar), that's Diabetes. The system is broken. Medicine helps fix the feedback loop.
