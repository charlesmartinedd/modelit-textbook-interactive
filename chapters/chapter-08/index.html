Weather & Climate

A butterfly in Brazil can actually affect the weather in Texas. Here's how.

Prediction is hard. Especially when it comes to the future. Meteorologists have supercomputers, but they still can't tell you if it will rain 3 weeks from now. Why?

Chaos Theory

It's not because they are bad at math. It's because the atmosphere is a Chaotic System. Small changes in the beginning result in massive differences later on.

Feedback Loops in Climate

Climate is different from weather. Climate is the average. But climate has feedback loops too:

  • Ice Albedo Effect: Ice melts -> Dark ocean exposed -> Absorbs more heat -> More ice melts. (Positive Feedback)
  • Cloud Cover: Hotter Earth -> More clouds -> Sunlight reflected -> Cooler Earth. (Negative Feedback)
